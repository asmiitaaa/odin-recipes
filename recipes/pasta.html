PASTA RECIPE

Description

Why buy the boxed stuff when you can make noodles at home? This homemade pasta recipe is way better than what you can buy at the store — and it’s surprisingly easy to throw together with just five ingredients you likely already have on hand!

You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below:

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Egg
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Make the dough
  2. Knead the dough
  3. Let it rest
  4. Use a pasta machine to roll and cut the dough into desired shapes.
